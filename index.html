About Us

Born from the vision of EA Enterprises and strengthened by S.M. Technologies, EA Global represents the union of engineering, animation, machine learning, and enterprise power. We exist to take what our subsidiaries create and bring it to the world stage — responsibly, ambitiously, and at scale.

Our Story

EA Global didn’t appear overnight — it was the inevitable evolution of a powerful vision shared by lifelong partners and builders.

2015 — EA Enterprises founded, focusing on high-performance hardware.

2018 — EA Parts created, securing industrial manufacturing pipelines.

2020 — S.M. Technologies emerges, reshaping 3D programming.

2023 — Joint venture SEAM Tech revolutionizes AI + animation workflows.

2025 — EA Global established, unifying expansion and international presence.
